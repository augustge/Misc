An approximation for differential equations with spatially dependent coefficients.
Given a differential equation of the form \[ \varepsilon \frac{d^ny}{dx^n} + a_{n-1}(x)\frac{d^{n-1}y}{dx^{n-1}} + ...+a_1(x)\frac{dy}{dx}+a_0(x)y = 0 \] assume a solution of the form \[ y(x) \simeq \exp \left[\frac{1}{\delta}\sum_{n=0}^\infty \delta^n S_n(x)\right] \]
